Tombili - The Stray Cat

Tombili was a beloved stray cat that lived in Istanbul. He became famous for his iconic pose while relaxing in a local teahouse.

Despite being a stray, Tombili captured the hearts of the locals and visitors alike. He became a symbol of the city's unique charm and the compassion people have for animals.

Unfortunately, Tombili passed away, but his memory lives on through the countless photos and the love of those who knew him.
